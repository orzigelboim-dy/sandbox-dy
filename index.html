And one more thing - Dynamic Links!

Dynamic Links let you optimize the target URL of any link. They’re great for ads, when you don't know where exactly to direct your traffic - to the homepage or to the category page? To a category page sorted by lowest price first, or highest price first? With Dynamic Links, you can choose as many target URL options as you wish and know that your traffic is automatically directed to the best one. Conversion data for each target URL is available to you in real time.
